European Union Cyber Laws

The Data Governance Act

The Data Governance act is a framework that creates structures to promote data sharing. It regulates international transfers of non-personal data, the reuse of public sector data, and a regime for data intermediaries. It also introduces data altruism organizations to collect and process data as well as the European Data Innovation Board.

Cyber Resilience Act

This proposed legislation introduces common cybersecurity rules for any item with digital components, including hardare and software.
