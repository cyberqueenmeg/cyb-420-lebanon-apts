Welcome to the Cyberwarfare Branch of Lebanon

As the head of Lebanon's Cyberwarfare Branch, I am proud to introduce our mission and capabilities. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to protect our nation's cyber infrastructure from evolving threats and to ensure our digital sovereignty.
