Lebanon's Cyber Team

Head of Cyberwarfare

The Lebanese Directorate of Intelligence under the Lebanese Armed Forces oversees national cybersecurity operations. However, the identity of the individual heading cyberwarfare is not publicly known.

Military-Government Cyber Subordinates

Various military and intelligence units under the Directorate of Intelligence specialize in cyber operations, though specific names of officers are not available publicly.

Cyber-Operatives: Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs)

APT34 (OilRig)

Aliases: OilRig, Greenbug, Helix Kitten

Skills & Specializations: Cyber espionage, especially in telecommunications, energy, and finance sectors in the Middle East. Focuses on data theft and surveillance for intelligence gathering.

Known Cyber-attack Methods: Spear-phishing, credential harvesting, web-based vulnerability exploitation, and the use of custom malware such as POWBAT and QUADAGENT.
