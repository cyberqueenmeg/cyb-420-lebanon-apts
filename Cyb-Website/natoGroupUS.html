    Operation Glowing Symphony

    Operation Glowing Symphony targeted ISIS’s global media networks, aiming to disrupt its propaganda and communication infrastructure. By infiltrating ISIS’s network, USCYBERCOM gained control over key accounts, removed propaganda materials, and disabled ISIS’s ability to operate online.

    USCYBERCOM’s motivation for this operation was twofold: to undermine ISIS’s recruitment, propaganda, and operational abilities and to disrupt their communication channels, which directly supported ground operations and reduced ISIS’s influence.
